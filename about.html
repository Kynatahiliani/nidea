about us

N'idea

Elegance Threads is a New Delhi-based boutique that celebrates the richness of Indian craftsmanship through a thoughtfully curated collection of ethnic and contemporary women’s wear. From handwoven sarees and intricately designed kurtis to elegant dresses and artisanal accessories, each piece is sourced directly from skilled artisans across India. Committed to sustainability and authenticity, Elegance Threads not only supports local craftsmanship but also offers a personalized shopping experience that helps every woman express her unique style with grace and confidence.
